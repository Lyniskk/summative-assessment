What is ULTRAKILL?

ULTRAKILL is a fast-paced ultraviolent retro-style FPS developed by Arsi "Hakita" Patala. Inspired by other shooters like Doom, Quake, and Devil May Cry, it has fast paced gameplay, unique weapon combos, and most importantly, SPEEDRUN STRATEGIES!!

The video below shows a video on Youtube by Kolta speedrunning through the level "7-4 // LIKE ANTENNAS TO HEAVEN". Keep in mind this is the FORMER world record, so there are faster times out there. (Side note, don't worry if you can't understand the gameplay. It's supposed to be confusing. Even I can't really figure out what's going on. Also, be wary of the volume, it's kind of loud.)

Fun fact: The official website for ULTRAKILL used to be "devilmayquake.com".
